About Us

AideWiser SolTek is a brainchild of two close friends and motivated professionals, highly experienced in their respective fields, who wisely decided to aid a spectrum of organisations to prosper! In the contemporary world, companies prefer to outsource functions in order to reduce cost, while getting things done efficiently. Our founders understood this and came up with an intelligent idea of AideWiser.

Our Story

The Founders always wanted to add value to people’s lives and help enhance quality of living through their work. With full time jobs, businesses and freelance consulting they were on the path they always wanted to be. One fine day during the COVID pandemic, they thought to set up their own establishment and dedicate themselves to the dream of becoming not only an aide but also wisely provide required support.

Vision

To be the right hand in everyone’s success

Mission

To provide organisations with expert solutions which help them achieve their goals and become
a leading partner, in enhancing business processes with next generation technologies.

Our Services

Staffing

We are just a call away for any kind of Human Capital requirements. With our unique tools and hiring solutions we have successfully bridged the skill gap that corporates face. We provide,

1.Recruitment (third party vendor services for bulk hiring)
2.Talent Management (head hunting for niche skills as well as Lateral Hiring)
3.Sub Contracting (open to work with other consultants on profit sharing model)
4.Placements (jobs to the freshers from college to their most desired MNCs)

We are in partnership with different Job Portals, Employment Exchanges, Labour Associations, Education Institutions, Government aided Skill Trainings, CSR funded Programs and NGOs to connect you with the best fit!
